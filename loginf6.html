About CaloriSense

CaloriSense is a smart diet planner that helps you know exactly **what to eat each day**. Based on your age, weight, and health goals, it provides **personalized daily meal plans** and keeps track of your eating habits. Stay consistent, monitor your progress, and achieve your fitness goals through healthy eating.

How It Works

Step 1

Create your profile with age, weight, and dietary goals.

Step 2

Get a **daily diet plan** with breakfast, lunch, dinner & snacks.

Step 3

Log what you eat daily and track calories/nutrition intake.

Step 4

See progress reports and adjust plans as your health improves.
